Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when using Ender List:

  • Account Information: When you sign in via Google or Discord, we receive your username, email address, and profile avatar from the OAuth provider. We do not receive or store your password.
  • Server Listings: Server name, IP address, description, and associated metadata you submit.
  • Voting Data: We record which users vote for which servers, along with timestamps and IP addresses for anti-abuse purposes.
  • Usage Data: Standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps).
  • Cookies: We use session cookies to maintain your login state. These are strictly functional and not used for advertising or tracking.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and maintain your session
  • To display and rank server listings
  • To prevent vote manipulation and abuse
  • To generate aggregate, anonymized statistics (total servers, total votes, etc.)
  • To enforce our Terms of Service

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law (subpoena, court order, legal process)
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of Ender List, our users, or the public
  • With your explicit consent

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. Server listings and vote records may be retained in anonymized form for statistical purposes.

5. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), hashed credentials (bcrypt), secure session management, and rate limiting. However, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Ender List is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to: (a) know what personal data we collect about you; (b) request deletion of your personal data; (c) opt out of the sale of personal data (we do not sell personal data); (d) not be discriminated against for exercising these rights.

8. International Users

If you access Ender List from outside the United States, your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using our service, you consent to this transfer.

9. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Self-hosted PostgreSQL — database infrastructure (hosted on our own servers)
  • Google OAuth / Discord OAuth — user authentication

These services have their own privacy policies which govern their use of your data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced on our website. Your continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy inquiries, data deletion requests, or questions about this policy, contact us via our Discord community or at the email address listed in our Terms of Service.
